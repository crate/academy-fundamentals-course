Congratulations – you've completed all the lessons for this course! Now it’s time to test your knowledge and earn a certificate of completion by taking the exam.  

The exam consists of a series of multiple-choice questions, each having one correct answer. There’s no time limit, and you’re free to refer to the course materials and CrateDB documentation as needed. 

You'll need to have the course example dataset loaded into your CrateDB cluster to answer some of the questions. 

If you need to load the data now, follow the setup process before going further. 

Once you’re happy with your answers for each question, press the “Submit” button. You’ll then see your score, along with feedback for any questions that you answered incorrectly.  

If you score 75% or higher, you’ll be able to claim your certificate of completion. Add your certificate to your LinkedIn profile and share it on your social media channels to show off your new skills! Don’t forget to tag us: 

  • LinkedIn 
  • X / Twitter 

Don’t worry if you didn’t achieve a passing grade. You can repeat the exam as many times as you like. 

Good luck! If you have questions or need assistance, join our online community where we’re ready to help you.
